About Me

My name is Santosh Shrestha. I moved to Canada about two years ago. It has been a big learning experience since then. I am in the Computer Programmer Analyst program here in Fanshawe.

A little about my background, I was born in Nepal, a small beautiful country in between India and China. I grew up in the capital city, Kathmandu. Nepal is very diverse. People speak more than 120 languages through out the country. It is very rich in culture and traditions. I love travelling and exploring new places. From very young, I loved hiking and trekking which is also the main source of income for many. Tourism is the main industry of Nepal, though it has been difficult in promoting tourism in the country after the massive earthquake in April 2015. Many people from different country are visiting Nepal in an effort to raise country's economy. Prince Harry visited Nepal recently in March 2016.
